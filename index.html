Visit Karachi

"Discover the vibrant energy, rich history, and stunning coastline of Karachi—Pakistan’s gateway to adventure and culture!"

A sprawling park located on the shore of the Arabian Sea, Bagh Ibn-e-Qasim is a popular spot for families and fitness enthusiasts. It has jogging tracks, playgrounds, and lush green lawns, making it the perfect place to unwind and relax.

A foodie’s paradise, Burns Road is a bustling street lined with shops and restaurants that serve mouth-watering Pakistani and Mughlai cuisine. From biryani to nihari, this street has something for everyone.

Located inside the historic Frere Hall building, the Frere Hall Library is a quiet and peaceful oasis in the heart of the city. The library has a vast collection of books and a beautiful reading room, making it the perfect place to escape the chaos of the city.
